About MBTI Bot World

MBTI Bot World is a creative and technical experiment to simulate emotional intelligence using the framework of the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI). Each bot embodies a distinct cognitive personality, allowing users to explore how thinking, feeling, intuition, and sensing manifest in real conversation.
